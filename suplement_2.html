Magnesium

Magnesium is an essential mineral for our body. Although many foods contain it, the poor quality of the land, increasingly malnourished and devitalized, causes it to have less magnesium.

Magnesium may help in the following functions:

Nourishes bones and muscles.

Regulate bowel function.

Balancing the nervous system.

Relax the blood vessels.

Regulate blood sugar levels.
